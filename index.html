01 PITCH & MARKET ANALYSIS
Elevator Pitch
"A minimalist 2D platformer where you play as a shadow that must outrun the rising light — die once and you restart the world, keeping only the scars."
Unique Selling Proposition
Permadeath with "scar memory" — environmental damage from your runs persists. Cracked floors stay cracked. Enemies remember your last approach. Death teaches the world, not just the player.
Permadeath+ Scar Memory Minimalist
Target Audience & Personas
Primary: "The Masochist" — 18–28, played Hollow Knight, Celeste. Wants challenge + story payoff.

Secondary: "The Aesthete" — 20–35, buys games for art direction. Clean visuals sell the download.

02 CORE DESIGN & THE 3 Cs
Character
Shadow silhouette, no face. Grows more detailed (cracks, tears) as runs accumulate. Communicates state via deformation, not UI. No health bar — posture tells HP.
Camera
Fixed 2D side-scroll. Camera leads player by 80px, lags by 40px. Orthographic, no zoom. Panic events trigger a subtle 2px shake at 12Hz. No parallax — single flat plane.
Controls
Move: WASD / D-pad. Jump: Space / A. Dash: Shift / RB (4s cooldown). Crouch: Ctrl / LB. Interact: E / X. No attack button — avoidance is the only verb.

Mechanics & Edge Cases
🌘
Light Avoidance
Player dissolves in direct light (1.2s grace window). Shadows and overhangs are safe zones. Light rays are dynamic — move after 30s to prevent camping.
⚠ Edge: player trapped between two converging rays → guaranteed death. Spawn rays with minimum 3-tile exit gap.
💨
Dash
4s cooldown. Invincible first 0.15s. Cannot dash into solid tiles — stops at wall edge. Air-dash resets on wall-touch.
⚠ Edge: corner-clipping exploit. Add 2px inward nudge on dash trajectory resolve.
🩹
Scar System
Each death flags tiles with visual wear (cracks, darkening). After 5 deaths on same tile: tile becomes permanently unstable (breaks underfoot). Max 30 scar markers per zone.
⚠ Edge: player intentionally scars all tiles to grief run. Cap scar density per 4×4 grid region.

04 TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE & ART PIPELINE
Engine & Tools
Engine: Unity 2022 LTS
Reason: Industry standard, robust 2D Universal Render Pipeline (URP) for dynamic lighting, and highly scalable C# architecture.
Packages: Cinemachine (camera tracking/shake), NodeCanvas (state machines for AI), 2D Light (URP).
Version control: Git + GitHub.
Technical Constraints
Target FPS: 60fps locked (PC), 30fps min (mobile future)
Resolution: 1280×720 base, pixel-perfect scaling
Memory budget: <200 MB RAM
Max draw calls: 80/frame
Platform: PC (Win/Mac) primary
Scar markers cap: 30 per zone (perf guard)
Art & Audio Guidelines — Color Palette
Mood: Liminal, quiet dread. Think ink-wash meets neon crack.
Audio style: Ambient drone base + piano stabs on danger. No music during boss — only heartbeat SFX.
Art style: 32×32 pixel sprites. 1-bit primary shapes + selective yellow highlight. No gradients on sprites.
Asset Priority List
  • MustPlayer shadow sprite (8 frame walk, 4 frame dash, death anim)
  • MustLight ray configuration (URP Freeform Light 2D)
  • MustZone 1 tileset (30 tiles: platform, wall, crumble variants)
  • MustScar overlay sprites (5 wear stages per tile)
  • ShouldZone 2–3 tilesets + parallax background layers
  • ShouldUI: XP counter, death counter HUD (Unity UI Toolkit)
  • ShouldAmbient audio tracks (4 zone variants)
  • CouldAnimated background entities (non-interactive)
  • CouldGamepad rumble on scar-tile break (Input System)
  • CouldSteam achievements (10 planned)
